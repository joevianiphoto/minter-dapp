Story

Meet Pit Puppy #0, Thumper.

As a pet lover, the thought of adding a pit bull to the home was never an option. All the misunderstandings and bad press these wonderful dogs receive had my eyes closed.

After searching and searching all the rescues and humane societies for a mixed breed boxer, we finally found one. She was a beautiful young brindle that was returned to the humane society twice. She was so friendly and exactly what we were looking for, so we gave her a chance.

After a few months, she started looking less like a boxer mix, so we sent off for a dna kit. The results came back as mostly Pit and some Jack Russel. Not exactly what we were looking for, but she has turned out to be the most wonderful addition the family.

After two years now, she brings nothing but fun and love and protection to the whole house, humans and pets alike, including two cats. She has never shown a vicious side, not even over her food bowl.

She was the inspiration for this collection, so all you will find are happy loving puppies ready for their new home in your collection.
